# Rust Dortmund Community

Rust Dortmund Meetup is a hybrid meetup for individuals working with Rust or eager to learn the Language. We welcome beginners, students, and seasoned developers from all fields. Most attendees are bilingual in English and German.

- You work with, or you tinker around with Rust? **You're at the right place!**
- Regular in-person meetups, streamed online, with an impulse talk or hands-on!
- Connecting Expertise from Dortmund to the World!
- Give people around the world an impression of our amazing City!
